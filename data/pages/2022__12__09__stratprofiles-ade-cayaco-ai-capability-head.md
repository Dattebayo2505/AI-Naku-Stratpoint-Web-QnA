StratProfiles: Ade Cayaco, AI Capability Head

*In StratProfiles, we get to know the Stratizens — their aspirations and inspirations. We hope that, like them, you grow your career with Stratpoint too.*

# **Ade Cayaco, AI Capability Head**

**What did you want to be when you were young?**

An engineer. I liked to dismantle toys as a kid. The problem was reassembling them! What I do in Stratpoint is not so different — I just do it in the software world. And I now know better how to find solutions to the problems.

**How did you end up as the AI Capability Head?**

I was fresh out of UP when I worked in Stratpoint. I started as a mobile developer, but I was also very interested in low-level or hardware programming. I used that to tackle more complex problems that dealt with the OS or kernel-level code. Then, I branched out to cross-platform development and desktop application development. I have a very curious nature, so AI, being an emerging tech, was something I was excited about. I took the plunge and learned how it worked. I even started a learning group and initiative within Stratpoint. With the support of management and our CEO, we started our AI laboratory unit, and I became the capability head.

**What do you think is IT’s role in making the world a better place?**

In the age of the internet, technology should be used to elevate our living standards. Recently, it has helped decentralize and democratize education by making knowledge available on-demand. That’s a great thing because technology should be accessible to everyone.

**What do you like most about being in Stratpoint?**

Stratpoint encourages its employees to take charge of their learning. We never stop being curious.

**Tech continuously evolves — how do you keep yourself updated?**

I read. I use social media for educational purposes by following trailblazers in the industry.

I execute. I pull code from Github and make it work in my own machine or environment.

I experiment. I break the code and apply it to my own use case.

**If you weren’t at Stratpoint where do you think you would be?**

I’d probably be in another engineering field — maybe robotics or mechanical engineering.

**Among popular tech personalities, who do you want to emulate and why?**

In the Marvel Universe, Tony Stark — creating Jarvis is a dream.

In our actual universe, Vinton Cerf and Bob Kahn. They are the computer scientists credited with creating the internet we know today.

**If you could invent/build any tech today, what would it be?**

A new form of battery technology:  environmentally friendly, cheap to manufacture, and with a larger capacity.

**What is your ultimate goal career-wise?**

Retire early by creating my own product or my own intellectual property.

**What are your pursuits outside of work?**

Relearning life through the eyes of my 1 year old son.

**What is your advice to younger professionals?**

Never stop being curious! Use programming languages as tools for problem solving (instead of worshiping them!).

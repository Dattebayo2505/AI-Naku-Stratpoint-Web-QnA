# Leading Silicon Valley Tech Enterprise Enhances Workday Implementation with Comprehensive QA Services

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Portfolio-workday-Header.webp "Portfolio-workday-Header")

Summary

A leading Silicon Valley (SV) tech company engaged Stratpoint for the former’s global implementation and updates of Workday, a top Human Capital Management (HCM) and Financial Management solution, for over 150,000 SV company employees. This resulted in streamlined testing processes and automated 93.9% of their test cases, ensuring smooth system updates and minimal operational disruption and empowering SV company’s business team to focus on core business activities.

Background

A leading Silicon Valley tech company faced a challenge with the global implementation and updates of Workday. With a user base of over 150,000 employees worldwide, the company needed to ensure seamless integration and minimal disruption during mandatory Workday releases.

Challenge

* **Mandatory Workday Releases:**  SV company had to accept changes and meet new requirements while supporting existing features.
* **Growing Complexity in Configuration:** Increasing customizations led to difficulties in maintaining a consistent QA environment and managing a growing list of features.
* **Inadequate Time for Testing:** With only six weeks to test changes before release, there was a risk of insufficient coverage and potential disruption of existing functionalities.
* **Limited UAT Capacity:** The business team struggled to conduct User Acceptance Testing (UAT) due to a lack of time and insufficient knowledge of best practices.

Solution

To address these challenges, Stratpoint deployed a dedicated team of over 50 QA and test automation engineers and implemented a comprehensive QA strategy over the past two years.

## HOW IT WORKS

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/UIUX-process.webp "UIUX-process")

**Discover**. Facilitate empathy sessions to discover and understand not just the problems users want to solve but also their hopes, abilities, and desires.

**Define**. Based on the problem, insight, and observations, define the scope of the design project and set the approach or direction.

**Ideate**. Through a collaborative effort between the business and development teams, conceptualize innovative solutions to address the identified problems.

**Prototype**. Turn ideas into concrete low and high-fidelity wireframes.

**Testing**. Conduct user testing to validate design ideas.

IMPORTANT ADVISORY

We would like to inform the public that Stratpoint does not offer investments to the general public. We also want to remind the public that Stratpoint does not recruit or offer employment outside its official website, official communication channels (e-mails) or validated recruitment platforms.

We urge everyone to help us prevent the fraudulent use of Stratpoint Technologies Inc. in malicious schemes that involve unauthorized investments disguised as job opportunities promising instant income.

For your protection, please avoid engaging in such activities and refrain from interacting with individuals who contact you through social media groups or other unauthorized channels. Instead, remain vigilant in reporting the posts and profiles so they can be blocked and flagged as scams.

We appreciate your support in safeguarding our brand and community.

*To report any suspicious posts or messages, please email: reportconcerns@stratpoint.com*

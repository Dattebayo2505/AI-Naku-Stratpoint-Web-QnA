Businesses rely on the uninterrupted flow of data to keep their operations running smoothly. Every minute of downtime could mean lost sales, delays in production, and compromised customer trust. That is why it is important for organizations to have a backup and disaster recovery strategy that addresses data loss, outages, and downtimes that involve not just the IT department but the entire organization.

**Topics**

* Modernizing Backup with Cloud
* Disaster Recovery Approaches
* Benefits of Different Backup and DR Strategies
* International and Local Customer Success Stories

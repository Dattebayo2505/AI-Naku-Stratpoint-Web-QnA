#### April 12, 2023

Designing Value with Roi Placente, UI/UX Designer

Meet Roi Placente, a UI/UX Designer at Stratpoint who believes that true design efficiency starts with a deep alignment with client goals and a commitment to asking the right questions.

#### 

#### 

#### **How do you help clients achieve their goals?**

First, I make sure I fully understand what the clients want to achieve. I process these goals into usable information to come up with concepts and wireframes. When I am fully aligned with my client’s goals, I can formulate my designs more quickly, efficiently, and accurately.

#### **How does your work contribute to empowering businesses?**

By creating designs that are easy to use, easy to understand, and functional, we can attract more users, more customers, and that translates into higher revenues. 

#### **How do you contribute to building or maintaining a good working relationship with clients?**

Collaboration and communication are key to having a good relationship with our clients. We listen to their ideas and challenges so we can come up with a user-friendly solution.

#### **How do you ensure that you always deliver value to your colleagues and clients?**

Do not assume. Ask questions if you don’t know. There’s no harm in asking questions rather than doing what you don’t know. I can also work fast and efficiently if I know what needs to be done.

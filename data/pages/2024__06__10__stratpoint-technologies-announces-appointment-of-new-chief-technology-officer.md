Stratpoint Technologies announces appointment of new Chief Technology Officer

June 01, 2024, Mandaluyong City — Stratpoint Technologies, enabler of digital transformation through software, quality assurance, Cloud, data, and AI, is pleased to announce the appointment of Zayd Tolentino as its new Chief Technology Officer (CTO). With a career spanning 15 years, Zayd brings not only tech expertise to the table but also a wealth of experience in digital transformation across startups and enterprises in various industries: **Fintech, InsurTech, Social Media, Telco, Logistics, Power Utility, HRTech**.

“We are thrilled to have Zayd in the Stratpoint executive team and tech council,” says Mary Rose dela Cruz, Stratpoint CEO. “His visionary approach to technology will be instrumental in positioning Stratpoint at the pole position of technology companies and, more importantly, delivering value to our customers.”

As the new CTO, Zayd will be responsible for spearheading technological strategy and innovations in Stratpoint and Stratpoint Venture Studios. He will oversee the development and implementation of cutting–edge technologies that will bring competitive advantage to Stratpoint’s customers. Through his leadership, Stratpoint expects to further elevate its solutions delivery and product building capabilities and continuously fulfill its commitment to showcase Filipino tech talent to the world.

“Beyond being experts in technology, I believe we have the potential to position Stratpoint as a recognized tech leader in the industries we serve. By deepening our understanding of each industry’s landscape and staying ahead of market trends, we can offer strategic insights and solutions that address our clients’ specific challenges and ultimately satisfy their end consumers. This dual focus on technology and industry expertise will add significant value to our offerings,”  says Zayd Tolentino.

“It’s exhilarating to live in the era of AI, but what amazes me is that we have barely scratched the surface. In the coming years, with AI, the Internet of Things, blockchain, AR, VR, and more — there are so many opportunities for invention and innovation. My goal is to apply these technologies as practical solutions to real-world challenges of our customers and our customers’ customers,”  Zayd adds.

“I look forward to this new journey, to embrace challenges, push boundaries, and to do crazy things! Let’s redefine the limits of what’s possible!” he concludes.

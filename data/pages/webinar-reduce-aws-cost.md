![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Reduce-AWS-costs-Header.webp "Reduce AWS costs - Header")

Shifting to AWS will give you the performance and security you need to keep the business running in a global and digital economy. As you migrate more of your workloads to the cloud, you also need to keep your AWS costs in check. In this webinar, you will learn about how cloud management tool nOps enables you to cut down on AWS spend while helping you maintain an infrastructure that is secure, reliable, efficient, and operationally excellent. Presented by Stratpoint, nOps, and AWS.

**Topics**

* Implementing a Well-Architected AWS to generate savings: Solutions and Case Study
* Cost Management Best Practices
* Cost-optimization Recommendations

**Highlights**

* Case study of local company who saved on AWS spend using nOps
* How to reduce cost with rightsizing, chargebacks, and managing unused resources on AWS

**Offer**

Free AWS health check by Stratpoint for all attendees

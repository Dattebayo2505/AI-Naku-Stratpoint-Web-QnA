## Summary

A digital infrastructure investment company initiated a digital platform project to combat escalating fraud and simplify the eKYC (Know-Your-Customer) application process for consumers, banks, utilities, and various sectors. Stratpoint built the service engine component to facilitate secure and streamlined processes.

## Challenge

As identity fraud grew in complexity and frequency, the company sought to address:

* Increasing instances of **fraud and identity theft.**
* High costs associated with **fragmented and inefficient verification processes.**
* **Loss of trust** among users and organizations.

## Solution

The company engaged Stratpoint to work on the  **service engine component**  or the integration layer of the platform.

* **Agile Development:** Stratpoint deployed an agile dedicated team to build the service engine, facilitating a secured and seamless integration to manage eKYC processes efficiently.
* **Managed Cloud:** Stratpoint designed, built, and managed AWS (Amazon Web Services) Cloud resources for the service engine to help orchestrate the different services and partners.

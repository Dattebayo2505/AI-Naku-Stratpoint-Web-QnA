## Summary

A leading low-cost commercial airline in the Philippines enhanced its QA processes by implementing Stratpoint’s Managed QA service, integrated with a Testing Center of Excellence (TCOE) framework. This provided centralized QA governance, resource efficiency, and cost savings.

## Challenges

* **Escalating testing demands** due to frequent updates across customer-facing web and mobile apps and internal systems.
* **Operational inefficiencies** from managing multiple vendors, leading to administrative burdens and inconsistent QA standards.
* **High costs** associated with an unsustainable staff augmentation approach.

## Solutions

Stratpoint deployed a team of 43 QA experts under its TCOE framework to streamline the airline’s QA demands.

* **Centralized QA governance** ensured consistent quality and streamlined processes.
* **Resource optimization** through a Managed QA model, supported by test automation and manual testing for both customer-facing and internal applications
* **Cost-effective operations** achieved by shifting from a multi-vendor staff augmentation setup to a Managed QA service with a TCOE framework.

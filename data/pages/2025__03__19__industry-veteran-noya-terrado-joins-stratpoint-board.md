Industry Veteran Noya Terrado Joins Stratpoint Board

Stratpoint Technologies, a leader in digital transformation and enterprise technology solutions, proudly announces the appointment of Nora “Noya” Kakilala Terrado as an Independent Board Member. A distinguished leader in IT-BPM, digital transformation, and enterprise technology, Noya brings a wealth of experience that will further strengthen Stratpoint’s strategic capabilities and innovation efforts.

For over 30 years, Noya has held executive leadership positions in both the private and public sectors. She is the Founder of Carelon Global Solutions Philippines and has served as President and Country Manager of Headstrong Philippines, Asia CFO and board member of James Martin & Co Philippines, and Regional Financial Controller for Southeast Asia for Circle International of the Harper Group. She also played a significant role in the government as the Undersecretary of the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI), where she promoted the Philippine innovation industries to the global market.

Noya’s addition to Stratpoint’s board aligns with the company’s expansion across local, regional, and US markets. *“With over 30 years of* *experience in scaling global businesses and driving innovation**, I have collaborated with companies to implement advanced technologies. This background equips me to contribute to Stratpoint’s growth and organizational health by providing balanced oversight, relevant advice, and strategic insights. Having worked with the executive management and founder, I trust their vision and am excited to be part of Stratpoint’s journey,”* Noya shared.

Stratpoint CEO MR Dela Cruz recognizes the value of Noya’s experience in scaling businesses and driving sustainable growth: *“Noya’s experience in global markets, her expertise in the healthcare domain, and her operational experience in running large service organizations fit well with Stratpoint’s growth objectives in local, regional, and US markets. Her insights will be invaluable in helping us scale our services teams sustainably.”*

Stratpoint Founder and Chairman Paco Sandejas also recognizes the impact of Noya’s addition to the board: *“Having an independent leader of Noya’s reputation and gravitas on our board is a game-changer for Stratpoint’s long-term vision. She brings an invaluable mix of strategic foresight, operational expertise, and a deep understanding of how businesses can leverage technology for sustainable growth. As Stratpoint enters its next phase of growth, Noya’s guidance will be instrumental in strengthening our governance, expanding our presence in new markets, and ensuring we continue to deliver world-class solutions to our clients.”* 

As a thought leader in digital transformation and innovation, Noya sees immense potential for Stratpoint to influence industry standards and shape the future of technology solutions, recognizing the company’s proprietary skills development program as a driving force in creating a pipeline of IT talent, boosting the country’s capability and capacity in advanced technologies. Further, Noya sees investments in Stratpoint’s Innovation Lab for research and development as paving the way for creating innovative technology solutions for all its global partners.

With this appointment, Stratpoint continues to deliver enterprise-grade technology solutions, scale its services globally, and foster a culture of innovation.

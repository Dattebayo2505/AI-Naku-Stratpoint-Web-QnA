Choosing the Right Tech Partner for your Digital Transformation Journey

The pressure is on for enterprises to innovate, scale, and deliver value seamlessly; however, it is essential for businesses to understand that digital transformation is not just about deploying technology, but also about aligning technology, strategy, and tools with your business goals.

Selecting the right digital transformation partner is critical for success. Seek a strategic tech partner who understands your business goals, provides tailored solutions, and demonstrates long-term commitment. Partnerships that work in synergy, combining technical capabilities with domain expertise and a collaborative strategic approach, ensure that technology becomes a growth enabler rather than just an operational layer.

IT Services: Increasing Scalability and Lowering Cloud Infrastructure Cost

A Europe-based digital support solutions company needed to optimize its AWS infrastructure to manage rising costs and improve Cloud efficiency. Utilities: Enhancing Water Meter Reading Accuracy with Smart OCR Technology

Stratpoint developed a scanning solution using a custom pipeline with object detection and optical character recognition (OCR) models locally deployed on Android-based devices. The solution effectively detects and reads the serial number and water consumption dial on various meter types, with pre-trained models adaptable to new meter faces. This prototype paves the way for production deployment, offering **improved accuracy, scalability,** and **efficiency** in meter readings.

Telco: Revolutionizing Payment Processing with a Scalable Orchestration Platform

A leading telecom provider in the Philippines faced challenges with a payment service that only supported a few channels, forcing customers to rely on over-the-counter transactions, which contradicted their digital transformation goals. Stratpoint’s agile dedicated team implemented a Cloud-native payment orchestration platform that enabled online payments across multiple channels and gateways, including various credit and debit options. The solution also allowed 24/7 transaction handling and robust reporting. As a result, the platform processed Php 1B in transactions monthly, **expanded** online **payment capabilities**, and saw a **300% increase** in **online transactions.**

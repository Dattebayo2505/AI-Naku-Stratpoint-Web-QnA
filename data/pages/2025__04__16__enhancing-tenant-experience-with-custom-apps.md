Enhancing Tenant Experience with Custom Apps

Tenants today, whether of residential or commercial spaces, expect speed and convenience from property services, from rent payments to maintenance requests. Without seamless digital experiences, property managers struggle with slow manual workflows, inefficient communication, and poor tenant satisfaction. Embracing custom applications that streamline and digitize processes enhances the overall tenant experience and fosters loyalty.

## **The Cost of Outdated Tenant Management**

Traditional tenant management often involves cumbersome manual processes, leading to inefficiencies and frustration.

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Enhancing-Tenant-Experience-with-Custom-Apps-Infographic.webp "Enhancing Tenant Experience with Custom Apps - Infographic")

## **Custom Apps as Catalysts for Transformation**

Custom applications offer a powerful solution to these challenges, enabling real estate firms and property management offices to automate processes, enhance communication, and improve the overall tenant experience. A [study](https://www.emerald.com/insight/content/doi/10.1108/pm-08-2022-0058/full/html) found that features such as visitor and security management via property management apps positively influenced residents’ satisfaction. Another [report](https://www.spikeglobal.com/2024/08/15/the-essential-role-of-resident-apps-in-property-management/) revealed that implementing a resident app led to a 25% increase in amenity utilization and a 46% rise in resident participation in community events, fostering a stronger sense of community.

Self-service lease and rent management

Empower tenants to manage their leases independently through the app, including lease renewals, information updates, and online rent payments.

![Invest in Proptech](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Enhancing-Tenant-Experience-with-Custom-Apps-Maintenance.png)

Digital maintenance requests and tracking

Track and schedule repairs with automated updates and notifications. Residents can submit maintenance requests online, attach photos, and receive real-time updates. This streamlines the maintenance process and ensures timely resolutions.​

![Invest in Proptech](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Enhancing-Tenant-Experience-with-Custom-Apps-Real-time-communication.png)

Real-time communication

Facilitate instant communication between tenants and property managers through in-app messaging for quick issue resolution, timely updates, and personalized interactions. Community announcements, emergency alerts, and direct messaging also improve transparency.

![Invest in Proptech](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Enhancing-Tenant-Experience-with-Custom-Apps-Automated-notification.png)

Automated notifications and reminders

Keep tenants informed with automated notifications for rent due dates, maintenance schedules, and policy updates, minimizing confusion and ensuring smooth operations.

![Invest in Proptech](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Enhancing-Tenant-Experience-with-Custom-Apps-Data-insights.png)

Data-driven insights

Leverage data analytics to gain valuable insights into tenant feedback, request patterns, and property performance. This data-driven approach enables property managers to optimize operations and make informed decisions.

## **The Impact of Custom Apps for Real Estate Firms**

Investing in a custom app for tenant services yields significant business benefits. 

* **Higher tenant satisfaction:** Enhanced communication and streamlined services lead to improved tenant experiences, resulting in higher retention rates and positive referrals.
* **Increased operational efficiency:** Automating routine tasks reduces the administrative burden on staff, allowing them to focus on strategic initiatives and elevating tenant engagement.
* **Smarter decisions:** Access to comprehensive data enables property managers to identify trends, forecast needs, and implement proactive strategies, ultimately improving property performance and profitability.

Hawaii-based [Two Seas Property Management](https://asiagrowthpartners.com/case-study/transforming-property-management-the-success-story-of-two-seas-property-management-with-appfolio/c6069) faced challenges in managing its portfolio of over 90 rental homes and condos using various software solutions and manual paperwork. Through a property management app to automate back office tasks and property management services to their residents, they achieved a 50% increase in units under management, a 90% reduction in time spent on owner reports, and an 80% decrease in time spent on tenant screening.

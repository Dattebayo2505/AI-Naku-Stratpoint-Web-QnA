AI Platform Software: The Secret Weapon for AI-Driven Digital Acceleration

In a [report by the International Data Corporation (IDC)](https://www.idc.com/getdoc.jsp?containerId=prUS52472424), global revenue for AI platform software will grow to $153 billion by 2028. This growth reflects a fundamental shift in how organizations approach digital transformation. AI platforms emerge as more than just a suite of tools — they are comprehensive ecosystems that revolutionize the development and deployment of AI solutions. By combining infrastructures, frameworks, and deployment tools, AI platforms enable businesses to develop, scale, and manage AI solutions seamlessly. But what’s behind this surge in demand? From unified AI frameworks to the rapid adoption of Cloud-based solutions, AI platforms are becoming indispensable for businesses looking to embed AI deeply into their digital transformation strategies and stay ahead of the curve.

# 

# **Engines of Growth**

According to IDC, the evolution of AI platform software is rooted in two major trends:

1. **Unified platforms for predictive AI and GenAI**  
   Unified AI platforms that support predictive analytics and GenAI are streamlining the development, deployment, and management of AI solutions. By enabling interoperability across tools and frameworks, these AI platforms can reduce complexity and accelerate innovation.

 

1. **Cloud-based deployments outpacing on-premise solutions**  
   Cloud-based deployments of AI platforms are growing faster than on-premise deployments. Cloud environments’ flexibility, scalability, and cost-efficiency make them ideal for training and deploying AI models that require vast amounts of computing power and data storage.

# **AI Platform Software Empowering Businesses**

In the digital age, staying ahead of the curve is paramount. AI platforms offer businesses a powerful toolkit to achieve this goal.

Stratpoint becomes an AWS Advanced Consulting Partner

2018 has really been a great year. Just last Friday, December 28, during the last day of work for the year, we received great news from our friends at Amazon Web Services, Inc. (AWS) – Stratpoint is now an AWS Advanced Consulting Partner in the Amazon Web Services Partner Network (APN).  
 

### About Stratpoint

Stratpoint has twenty (20) years of experience in software development for enterprise and consumer markets. Stratpoint successfully developed and delivered mobile and web applications and solutions for clients in different industries such as telecommunications, retail, manufacturing, and finance.

Stratpoint is the first to deploy a Serverless Microarchitecture in the Philippines, using the AWS Lambda technology by Amazon Web Services. It allows the design of independent small components that form the application. Stratpoint also develops a comprehensive range of enterprise-grade solutions on Amazon Web Services that provide highly scalable, reliable, and secure end-customer user experiences.  
 

### What is the AWS Partner Network (APN)?

The AWS Partner Network (APN) is the global partner program of AWS. It is focused on helping APN Partners build successful AWS-based businesses or solutions by providing business, technical, marketing, and go-to-market support.

APN Consulting Partners are professional service firms that help customers of all sizes design, architect, build, migrate, and manage their workloads and applications on AWS.

![aws consulting journey](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/aws-consulting-partner-journey-1024x836-1-copy.webp "aws-consulting-partner-journey-1024x836-1 copy")

APN Partners are focused on their clients’ success, helping customers take full advantage of all the business benefits that AWS has to offer. With their deep expertise on AWS, APN Partners are uniquely positioned to help their clients at any stage of their Cloud Adoption Journey and to help them achieve their business objectives.

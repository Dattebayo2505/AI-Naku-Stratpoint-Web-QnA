Unlocking Smarter Finance with Real-Time Data Insights

In today’s fast-paced and highly competitive financial landscape, building data strategies in financial services is a pivotal asset. Data is the lifeblood of informed decision-making, but many financial service institutions are drowning in a sea of data quality issues, legacy systems, and siloed departments. A [report](https://mosaicsmartdata.com/tackling-data-health-to-enable-analytics-in-front-office-investment-banking/) highlights that 66% of investment banks struggle with data quality and integrity, and 83% of these banks do not have access to real-time data analytics. These challenges impede informed decision-making and can compromise regulatory compliance, highlighting a critical need: building data strategies in financial services that address current challenges and unlock the power of real-time insights.

# **Success in Action: Enhancing Digital Banking through Data Modernization**

A leading bank in the Philippines faced high data latency and fragmented access, hindering its digital services. The bank collaborated with [Stratpoint](https://stratpoint.com/)’s data and Cloud engineers to modernize its infrastructure and set up the baseline infrastructure for a robust data platform. The results were transformative, including reduced latency from 12 seconds to 1.7 seconds, centralized data management, improved scalability, and real-time data access. This enabled the bank to offer enhanced digital banking services and improve customer satisfaction.

## Summary

To accelerate their 2025 digital roadmap, the Philippines’ leading low-cost commercial airline strengthened their mobile development operations for their flagship booking mobile app. Facing delays, excessive bugs, and performance issues, the airline onboarded long-time tech partner Stratpoint to deliver mobile managed services, resulting in faster development, improved app stability, and a mobile experience aligned with the airline’s vision of delivering a seamless digital experience for millions of travelers.

## Challenges

As the airline’s primary revenue-generating touchpoint, the booking mobile app had become increasingly difficult to maintain and evolve. Key challenges included:

* **High defect rates**, exceeding the 2–3% bug threshold
* **Growing development demands** outpacing existing capacity
* **Misalignment** between the incumbent vendor and the airline’s internal IT

If left unresolved, these issues risk delaying releases, compounding technical debt, and weakening the digital customer experience at a critical time.

## Solution

The airline engaged Stratpoint for mobile managed services covering:

* **Skilled resources**, including iOS and Android developers, QA engineers, and tech leads
* **Rapid deployment**, with full team mobilization achieved in under one month
* **Restructured agile squads** , shifting from platform-based teams to cross-functional units aligned with customer journeys
* **Stratpoint developer leads**  joining the airline’s tech governance to advise on architecture and integration strategy
* **App optimization**  focused on technical debt reduction and smoother delivery cycles

Stratpoint brought in proven experience in mobile development and QA, a strong delivery framework, and cost-effective scalability.

KEY INITIATIVES

#FastForwardHER   
 Initiatives

This is how we drive change, empower women, and shape the future of tech one initiative at a time.

Stella Bootcamp

Stella Bootcamp

The Stella Program is a specialized leadership track dedicated to empowering and advancing our high-potential female leaders. It focuses on strategic thinking, impactful communication, team development, influential leadership, and values-driven guidance, preparing participants for expanded leadership roles.

Stella Rising

Stella Rising

The Stella Rising Mentorship Program is the capstone experience of the Manager Bootcamp: Star Path, designed to accelerate the career trajectory of its high-potential female leaders. This program evolves the simple 30/60/90-day check-ins from Batch 1 into a strategic, 6-month leadership accelerator that directly supports Stratpoint's Women Empowerment initiative.

All Female Internships

All Female Internships

Our all-female internships equip future tech leaders with mentorship, real-world experience, and the edge to succeed in tech careers.

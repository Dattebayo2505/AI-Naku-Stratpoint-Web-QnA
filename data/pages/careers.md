## WHY STRATPOINT?

![Careers - Why Stratpoint](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Why-Stratpoint-Careers-scaled-1.webp "Why-Stratpoint-Careers-scaled")

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Careers-why-1.png)
Silicon Valley mindset

Our founders have instilled in us their Silicon Valley mindset: fast, agile, and passion-driven. Enjoy autonomy, lack of bureaucracy, and freedom to experiment — without the chaos. We’re like a startup but with adult supervision.

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Careers-why-2.png)
Growth environment

Thrive during challenges and let your IT career flourish, under the mentorship of technology and business veterans. Expand your skills and deepen your knowledge through Stratpoint University and Architect Academy. Learn from your mentors’ experiences to enrich your own expertise.

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Careers-why-3.png)
Hybrid work

Hybrid is our mode of work. We deliver quality output while living our best lives.

When we do need to come together, we have inviting open work spaces designed to inspire collaboration and camaraderie.

## Training Programs

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/StratU-scaled-1.webp "StratU-scaled")

### Stratpoint University

Each Stratizen has a development plan that leads to long term success. Take self-paced and instructor-led courses, get your certifications, and propel your career forward!

### Architect Academy

Through an advanced and highly technical program, level up your skills as an engineer to that of a solution architect.

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Architect-Academy-scaled-1.webp "Architect-Academy-scaled")

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Management-Training-1.webp "Management-Training-1")

### Management Training

This accelerated program grooms fresh graduates into future sales leaders. Quickly gain expertise and experience in various fields, and jumpstart a fruitful IT career.

### Cadetship Program

This program aims to develop technical professionals in ReactJS, NodeJS, iOS, Android, OutSystems, Cloud, Data, and QA through an extensive training and mentorship. Work on high-value projects alongside experienced professionals. Be eligible for promotion in less than 2 years.

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Cadetship-scaled-1.webp "Cadetship-scaled")

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Internship-Program.webp "Internship-Program")

### Internship Program

As a recent graduate, gain valuable work experience in your field of interest. Become part of a team, learn from leaders, and grow both personally and professionally.

Stratpoint Technologies Awarded a 2026 Regional AWS Partner Award

*Stratpoint recognized as AWS Partner of the Year Philippines winner, one of many AWS Partners around the globe that help their customers drive innovation*

**Mandaluyong City, Philippines – 7th May, 2026** – [**Stratpoint Technologies**](https://stratpoint.com/), a leading digital transformation enabler and IT outsourcing partner in the Philippines, is excited to announce it is a recipient of a **2026 Regional AWS Partner Award – Philippines**, recognizing leaders around the globe that are playing key roles in helping their customers drive innovation and build solutions on Amazon Web Services (AWS). Stratpoint is excited to announce it has been named the winner of **Consulting Partner of the Year for the Philippines**, recognizing top AWS Consulting Partners that have provided significant contributions related to revenue, launched opportunities, net new certified individuals, and AWS designations earned.

Announced during the AWS Partner Summit in Singapore, the Regional AWS Partner Awards recognize AWS Partners that have embraced specialization, innovation, and cooperation over the past year. Regional AWS Partner Awards recognize partners whose business models continue to evolve and thrive on AWS as they support their customers.

Stratpoint named as PH’s First Landing Zone Certified Partner of Alibaba Cloud

[Stratpoint Technologies](https://stratpoint.com/), a leading digital transformation enabler in the Philippines, proudly announces its latest milestone as the first **Landing Zone Certified Partner** of [Alibaba Cloud](https://www.alibabacloud.com/) in the Philippines. This significant achievement highlights Stratpoint’s commitment to excellence and innovation in Cloud solutions.

The journey to this certification began with Alibaba Cloud’s search for strategic partners to expand their Cloud offerings in the Philippines. Stratpoint’s proven capability in implementing Landing Zone solutions with different Cloud providers positioned the company as a strong candidate. After rigorous assessments, Stratpoint’s Cloud experts were awarded as Alibaba Cloud Certified Professionals and have successfully built a Landing Zone tailored to Alibaba Cloud’s required use case.

“We are honored to be the first in the Philippines to achieve Alibaba Cloud Landing Zone certification,” said Sonny Carlos, Stratpoint’s Head of Cloud and Data Business. “This certification strengthens our ability to provide world-class infrastructure solutions to businesses in the Philippines. By becoming the first Alibaba Cloud Landing Zone Certified Partner, we can now offer our clients a strategic pathway to innovation in their Cloud journey that is tailored to their needs,” he added.

This prestigious certification is a testament to Stratpoint’s expertise in Cloud implementation and its ability to meet Alibaba Cloud’s rigorous standards. As a Landing Zone Certified Partner, Stratpoint is now uniquely positioned to deliver Alibaba Cloud’s Landing Zone solutions to enterprises in the Philippines, ensuring a secure, scalable, and efficient Cloud infrastructure.

Allen Guo, General Manager for the Philippines, Alibaba Cloud Intelligence, pointed out that Stratpoint’s technical expertise in Cloud architecture and deployment, particularly within the Alibaba Cloud ecosystem, made the company a standout choice. 

“Their broad range of service offerings, from consulting to technical support, showcases a well-established Cloud practice capable of supporting clients at every stage of their Cloud journey. Stratpoint’s alignment with Alibaba Cloud’s vision and strategic goals combined with a strong reputation in the local market further solidified their selection. Their capabilities in automation, innovation, and driving customer success made them an ideal local partner to promote and implement Landing Zone solutions,“ said Mr. Guo.

In relation to Alibaba Cloud’s development in the Philippines, Stratpoint being awarded as their first Landing Zone Certified Partner in the country is strategic in accelerating Alibaba Cloud’s market penetration through a trusted local partner, enhancing Alibaba Cloud’s technology and solutions, and underscoring the company’s commitment to the local market. 

As Stratpoint implements and promotes Landing Zone solutions in the Philippines, Alibaba Cloud will support the company with technical training and marketing resources. 

With this milestone, Stratpoint is poised to pave the way in delivering cutting-edge Cloud solutions that empower Filipino businesses to thrive in the digital age. The recognition also reinforces Stratpoint’s position as a trusted partner for organizations looking to harness the power of Alibaba Cloud’s advanced technologies.

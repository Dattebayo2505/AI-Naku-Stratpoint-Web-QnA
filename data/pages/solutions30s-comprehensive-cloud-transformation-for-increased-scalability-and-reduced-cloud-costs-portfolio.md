## Summary

Solutions30, a leading European provider of digital support solutions, partnered with Stratpoint to successfully transform and optimize their Cloud infrastructure, reducing monthly Cloud spend by 24.5%, enhancing Cloud security, and improving scalability, delivering a ~40% cost savings compared to staying on-premise.

## Challenges

As part of its evolving Cloud strategy, Solutions30 identified opportunities to align AWS Cloud spend more closely with business growth and usage trends. Solutions30 identified areas for enhancement in its Cloud infrastructure to unlock greater efficiency and agility:

* The potential for automating workload scaling
* Improving resource utilization
* Implementing a more centralized monitoring solution

With modernization at the top of mind, Solutions30 leveraged the natural refresh cycle of its on-prem infrastructure to accelerate its Cloud-first strategy. Transitioning from on-premise would allow Solutions30 to address key areas, including:

* Optimizing data security
* Reducing maintenance costs
* Minimizing the risk of downtime

## Solutions

Stratpoint, an AWS Advanced Consulting Partner, delivered a multi-faceted Cloud transformation for the client, which included:

![Invest in Proptech](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Solutions30-Cloud-Nav.png)

Cloud Consultancy & Optimization (Cloud Nav) 

A detailed assessment of Solutions30’s AWS environment identified cost-saving opportunities and inefficiencies, leading to a strategic roadmap for Cloud optimization.

![Invest in Proptech](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Solutions30-Cloud-Runway.png)

Landing Zone Implementation (Cloud Runway) 

Stratpoint re-architected Solutions30’s AWS infrastructure to establish a secure, scalable AWS Landing Zone, establishing a robust foundation for Cloud operations.

![Invest in Proptech](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Solutions30-Cloud-Autopilot.png)

Platform Engineering & DevOps (Cloud Autopilot) 

Stratpoint integrated CI/CD pipelines, automated scaling with Kubernetes, and implemented open-source tools like Grafana and Karpenter, reducing manual intervention and streamlining operations.

![Invest in Proptech](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Solutions30-One-Cloud.png)

One Cloud 

This initiative unified Solutions30’s Cloud management and enhanced security, significantly improving their AWS security score and operational efficiency.

![Invest in Proptech](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Solutions30-Cloud-Takeoff.png)

Migration (Cloud Takeoff) 

A meticulous data center exit strategy was executed for Solutions30’s France, Spain, Italy, and Benelux (Belgium, Netherlands, and Luxembourg) systems to migrate 139 on-premise servers to AWS, using lift-and-shift and traditional migration approaches to minimize disruption.

![Invest in Proptech](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Solutions30-Cloud-Cruise.png)

Managed Service (Cloud Cruise) 

Post-migration, Stratpoint provides continuous support through Cloud Managed Service, ensuring 24/7 monitoring, proactive maintenance, and ongoing optimization.

For banking leaders, the challenge isn’t just building APIs, but managing the rising costs and operational friction of scaling them. Traditional project-based API delivery often leads to over-engineered ghost features that drain resources without delivering value.

**API as a Product: A Lean Engineering Strategy for Banking Efficiency** explores how banks can shift from a project mindset to a product mindset. By reframing the API lifecycle as a strategic business engine, banks can maximize ROI and transform the integration layer into a primary driver of innovation.

**Download now to learn about:**

* Identifying and decommissioning ghost features that consume operational budget.
* How to use logical traffic clustering to prevent a single overloaded gateway from driving up compute spend.
* Leveraging lightweight data planes and hybrid deployment to scale based on reality, not theory.
* Moving from manual audit loops to machine-readable governance embedded directly in the platform.

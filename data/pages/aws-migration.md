## The time to make your move to the cloud is today.

In the last 12 months, we have seen the urgency and importance of being a cloud-enabled organization. By going to the cloud, companies can realize the following benefits:

![savings](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/icons_migration-01.png)

#### Savings

Shifting from CAPEX to OPEX, moving to the cloud allows you to only pay for what you use. Plus, no more hardware maintenance!

![performance](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/icons_migration-02.png)

#### Performance

With a growing global cloud infrastructure, you can expect improved latency, enabling better performance and fewer downtimes.

![scalability](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/icons_migration-03.png)

#### Scalability

Increase your capacity on-demand, and scale back down whenever you need to.

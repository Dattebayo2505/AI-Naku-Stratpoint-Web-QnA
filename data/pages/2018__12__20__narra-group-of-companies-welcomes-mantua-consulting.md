Narra Group of Companies Welcomes Mantua Consulting

[Mantua Consulting](https://mantuaservices.com/) just held its Christmas Party last December 18, 2018, at the Makati Shangri-La Hotel. One of the guests invited is the Chief Executive Officer of [Stratpoint Technologies, Inc](https://stratpoint.com/), MR Dela Cruz.

![MR at Matua](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/IMG_2094.webp "IMG_2094")

Mantua Consulting is an IT consulting company that offers planning and implementation of IT projects, and information security standards compliance. They offer a variety of services such as Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT), code review, phishing test, security implementation, and training.

According to MR, Stratpoint is deepening its partnership with Mantua through a strategic investment in Mantua Consulting.

Stratpoint will be welcoming Mantua Consulting into the Narra Group of Companies in 2019.

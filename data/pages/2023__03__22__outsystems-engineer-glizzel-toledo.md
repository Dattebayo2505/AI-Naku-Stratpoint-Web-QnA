#### March 22, 2023

Fostering Excellence and Collaboration with OutSystems Engineer Glizzel Toledo

#### **How do you help clients achieve their goals?**

When I deliver my tasks, I ensure that they pass the most rigid quality standards. In instances when I know that our deliverables may be compromised, I contribute solutions and suggestions on how we can maintain excellent output.

#### **How do you contribute to building or maintaining a good working relationship with clients?**

I believe that open communication is important in building and maintaining good relationships. Challenges can be resolved quickly when team members communicate honestly. From my end, I generously provide feedback and suggestions, and I expect my colleagues and clients to do the same. 

#### **How do you ensure that you always deliver value to your colleagues and clients?**

I read carefully and make sure I understand instructions given to me before I set to work. I make clarifications and anticipate potential issues. This way, my output will be aligned with the expectations of my team and the client.

#### **How do you help contribute to Stratpoint’s purpose of enriching lives, empowering businesses, and uplifting communities?**

Being a software engineer is more than a job. It is my craft, and I am passionate about excelling and making an impact. I help people by making their everyday tasks more convenient through apps. I share knowledge and insights with my colleagues and clients so we can achieve our goals and grow together as technology professionals.

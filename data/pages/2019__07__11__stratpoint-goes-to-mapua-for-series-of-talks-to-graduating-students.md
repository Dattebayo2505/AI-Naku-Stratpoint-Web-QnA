Stratpoint Goes to Mapua University for Series of Talks to Graduating Students

Yesterday, July 10, Kevin, Pao, and Lyndon were invited to the Mapua University Makati Campus to give graduating students a series of technical talks. These graduating students are from different programs such as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, and Bachelor of Science in Information Systems.

Lyndon introduced Test Automation, where he discussed how it is being used in the industry. Pao talked about the State of Mobile in 2019, while Kevin talked about Getting Started with AWS.

This is not Kevin’s first time to talk about AWS. He discussed the same topic at the Mapua iCpEP.SE week event at the Mapua University Intramuros Campus. Together with him then were Ade, who discussed the State of AI, and Sarah, who discussed Getting Started with Flutter.

![Strat Goes To Mapua](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Stratpoint-Goes-to-Mapua.webp "Stratpoint-Goes-to-Mapua")

In the event, Stratpoint also opened its doors for students of Mapua University who were interested to [join Stratpoint](https://stratpoint.com/careers/). Links where they can apply were posted during the talks.

We at Stratpoint make sure that we are always one step ahead on emerging technologies. We take knowledge sharing as an art form. We want to inspire students to be the best in the industry.

We hope you all learned a lot yesterday. Thank you for inviting us, Mapuans! Until next time!

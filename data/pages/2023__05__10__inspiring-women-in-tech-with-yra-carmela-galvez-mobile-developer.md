#### May 10, 2023

Inspiring Women in Tech with Yra Carmela Galvez, Mobile Developer

#### **How do you help clients achieve their goals?**

I help clients achieve their business goals by providing good quality software optimized for their business needs. I listen to their needs and respond accordingly.

#### **How does your work impact communities?**

I am one of the relatively few women in this male-dominated field. I can motivate other young women to pursue this career by showing them that women can equally thrive in software development.

#### **Please share a memorable accomplishment as an engineer at Stratpoint.**

I was one of the developers who worked on SM’s online application. The app helped business owners connect with their consumers seamlessly during the pandemic. The app won the 2022 AIBP ASEAN Enterprise Innovation Award. I feel proud and fortunate to have contributed to this project.

#### **How do you maintain a good working relationship with clients?**

I always treat others with respect and strive to earn the same.

#### **How do you ensure that you always deliver value to your colleagues and clients?**

To clients, I help deliver high-quality products and services promptly. To my peers, I make sure to complete tasks on time and contribute to the timely completion of the project.

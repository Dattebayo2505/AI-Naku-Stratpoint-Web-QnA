## **Agentic AI in Action**

Agentic AI is already redefining industries with innovative applications:

## **Challenges and Considerations in Adopting Agentic AI**

While Agentic AI offers immense benefits, it’s important to acknowledge potential challenges, including data quality and accessibility, where high-quality and readily available data is required for effective Agentic AI implementation. Organizations relying on legacy systems can also require careful planning and effort to integrate Agentic AI with existing systems. Companies should also ensure the responsible development and deployment of Agentic AI to ensure fairness, transparency, and accountability, and avoid bias. Many organizations may also lack the necessary skills and knowledge to build and manage AI projects, including Agentic AI.

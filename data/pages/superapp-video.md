What makes a super app, SUPER? In this webinar, learn what differentiates a super app from a regular app and understand what it takes to build one.

**Topics:**

* What Makes an App Super?
* Making a Super App Fly: Build Super Apps Using Design Thinking
* Overcoming Challenges in the Flight: Architecture Design
* Takeaways: Leveraging a Super App

**Highlights:**

* How to build your core business ecosystem in one app to address customer needs
* Digital transformation frameworks and how you can achieve business goals through technology and culture
* Scaling your business with product roadmap and vision

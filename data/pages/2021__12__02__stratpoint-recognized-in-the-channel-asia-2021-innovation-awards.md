Stratpoint recognized in the Channel Asia 2021 Innovation Awards

Capping an eventful 2021, Stratpoint was named a finalist in the Tech Innovation Award for Cloud in the Channel Asia 2021 Innovation Awards. Over 300 nominations from Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, Hong Kong, and the Philippines were submitted across 8 winner categories. 
The Innovation Awards 2021, according to award host Channel Asia, is the market-leading awards program for celebrating ecosystem innovation and excellence across the technology sector in Southeast Asia. Channel Asia raises the bar of industry awards through celebrating not only expertise but also transformative works by its nominees and awardees.
Stratpoint’s recognition is in the Tech Innovation – Cloud category, which, as described by the awarding body, “recognizes partners leveraging established and emerging solutions to enhance customer innovation levels, underpinned by deep levels of expertise and specialist services.”
As proof of Stratpoint’s expertise and transformative work, a top telecommunications company has tapped Stratpoint to handle the implementation, management, and support of their AWS environment. Only 100 days into the engagement, Stratpoint cut the average request completion waiting time by half and saved over 200K USD due to cost optimization, among other benefits. With Stratpoint efficiently managing the client’s AWS environment, the telco company stayed focused on creating new applications, platforms, and services that will elevate the experiences of both their lifestyle and enterprise customers.
As an Advanced Consulting Partner and Solutions Provider of the major cloud platform AWS, Stratpoint helps companies finally migrate their business to the cloud. With more than a decade of experience in cloud services, they bring best practices and templates to every cloud project, making the move to the cloud easy and cost-effective. Stratpoint offers app modernization, DevOps consultancy, cloud migration, and managed cloud services. They also carry the cloud optimization solution nOps, which ensures their client’s AWS is well architected, secure, and cost-efficient. 
Stratpoint is honored to be named part of the creme de la creme by Channel Asia, and vows to continue good work that will positively impact its clients, the industry, and the nation.

## Summary

A major Philippine telco ensured the stability and enhanced the capabilities of its payment orchestration platform through the Payment Modernization project, resulting in streamlined payment processing, increased revenue opportunities, and enhanced operational efficiency. The project, with the help of Stratpoint’s agile dedicated team, involved upgrading core technologies, integrating a new payment gateway, and implementing additional features to improve the overall payment experience.

## Challenges

The impending deprecation of key services and the need to replace an existing payment gateway pose a risk to the stability and functionality of the payment orchestration platform. The telco needed to act quickly to prevent potential system downtime, security vulnerabilities, and a negative impact on customer satisfaction.

## Solutions

Stratpoint’s agile dedicated team executed comprehensive version and non-version upgrades and transitions, including:

* Upgrading the payment orchestration platform to the latest versions of the key services.
* Seamless integration of the new payment gateway.
* Introducing a new functionality for customer service representatives to eliminate the need for direct access to payment gateway portals.
* Adding payment mode identifiers for streamlined reconciliation.
* Enhancing the payment orchestration platform’s web tool for Merchant ID configuration.

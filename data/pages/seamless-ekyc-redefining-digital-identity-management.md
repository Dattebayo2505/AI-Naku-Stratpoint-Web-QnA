A digital infrastructure investment company aimed to address a nationwide issue of escalating fraud and inefficiencies in eKYC (Know-Your-Customer) processes. Their vision was to establish a secure and streamlined platform for a simpler eKYC application process by having a digital platform for identity management between consumers, billers, and verification authorities. 

Through agile dedicated software development and managed Cloud solutions, Stratpoint built and managed the service engine component of client’s digital platform initiative, a significant step toward fighting fraud and becoming trusted stewards of sensitive consumer data.

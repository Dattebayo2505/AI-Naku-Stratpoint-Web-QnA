Stratpoint Advances QA Excellence with ISTQB® Gold Partnership

[Stratpoint Technologies](https://stratpoint.com/), a leading digital transformation enabler in the Philippines, is proud to announce that it is now part of the Gold Partnership Program of the [International Software Testing Qualifications Board (ISTQB®)](https://partner.istqb.org/), the world’s leading software test qualification standard organization. Being one of the select ISTQB® partners in the Philippines, this milestone affirms Stratpoint’s dedication to developing and maintaining the highest standards in software testing and quality assurance (QA). 

As part of the ISTQB® [Gold Partnership Program](https://www.phstqb.org/our-partners/), Stratpoint demonstrates its commitment to professional excellence by supporting its QA team’s continuous improvement and dedication to software testing certifications. This milestone is awarded to organizations that actively foster a culture of quality and technical proficiency. Eligibility for the Gold Partnership Program is awarded by achieving at least 14 certification points earned through certificates (with at least one Advanced Level Certificate) held by employees or external consultants who provide services for more than 70% of their time. These certification points validate a professional’s capability to perform complex testing tasks, understand quality management practices, and implement sophisticated testing strategies.

Becoming part of the Gold Partnership Program elevates Stratpoint’s QA capabilities, assuring clients of working with an elite team committed to quality excellence. This positions Stratpoint among the top organizations recognized for their QA expertise and ability to stay ahead of the curve on emerging standards in software testing.

*“Through our ISTQB®-certified QA professionals, Stratpoint ensures a globally recognized standard of quality and expertise in testing. This certification signifies that our QA team possesses validated skills in QA methodologies and best practices, reassuring our clients of consistent, reliable, and high-quality testing services,”* said Lyndon Catalan, Stratpoint’s Quality Assurance Business Unit Head. 

Stratpoint invests in the continuous professional development of its QA team, prioritizing certifications and upskilling to ensure alignment with industry standards. This commitment to ISTQB® certification fosters a culture of learning and positions Stratpoint as a trusted partner for organizations that value quality and efficiency in software development. By combining foundational knowledge with advanced training in areas like Test Automation, Performance Testing, and AI-driven QA, Stratpoint empowers its team to handle complex projects, contribute meaningfully to clients’ success, and advance their careers.

Lyndon’s vision for Stratpoint’s QA team is to lead with excellence and innovation, setting industry standards while positively impacting society. *“Beyond delivering high-quality, efficient solutions for our clients, I see our team as contributors to building inclusive and accessible technology that benefits communities. By continuously advancing our skills through certifications, embracing automation and AI, and fostering cross-functional collaboration, we will drive client success and also influence how technology improves everyday lives. Our commitment to quality is not just about meeting standards; it’s about making a meaningful difference in the digital world and contributing to a more connected, sustainable future. At the end of the day, quality starts with us.”* Lyndon concluded.

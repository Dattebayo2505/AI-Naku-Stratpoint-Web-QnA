# About Us

We fuel **digital acceleration**.

Through our capabilities in **software development, quality assurance, cloud, data, and AI,** we enable digital transformation at speed. We help execute the digital strategies of companies ready to transform and make sure we help them get through every transition smoothly and successfully.

We use technology to **enrich experience, empower business,** and **uplift communities.**

We foster **creativity, agility, reliability,** and **evolution**.

**We are Stratpoint.**

## WE ARE STRATPOINT

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/We-Are-Stratpoint-scaled-1.webp "We-Are-Stratpoint-scaled")

Stratpoint enables digital transformation, delivering enterprise-grade solutions globally for more than 25 years. We specialize in agile software development, cloud, data, and AI.

Our elite team of technology leaders helps businesses to rapidly adapt to the demands of today’s digital economy. We build modern applications to enrich experiences, empower businesses, uplift communities, and improve lives.

## OUR STORY



#### 2005

Stratpoint was founded in 2005 as a venue to showcase Filipino engineering talent on the world stage. An established software house called Sequel Solutions was acquired and rebranded into Stratpoint Technologies.



#### 2009

Booked its first Silicon Valley client.



#### 2010

Became the first homegrown AWS services provider in the Philippines.



#### 2013

Formed the Stratpoint Tech Council, a core group of experts who steer the company’s technology strategy and spearhead innovations.



#### 2017

Launched Stratpoint Labs, a business unit that covers artificial intelligence, machine learning, and emerging technologies.

## OUR LEADERS

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Paco.png)

## Paco Sandejas

Executive Chairman

Paco launched Stratpoint in 2005 when he acquired and rebranded Sequel Solutions. He is a technopreneur involved in ventures like Narra Venture Capital and PhilDev Foundation. He completed his PhD in Electrical Engineering at Stanford, where he was the co-inventor of the Grating Light Valve, one of Stanford’s top IP revenue generators. He is also a board member of Sunlife and Maya Bank.

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/MR-img.png)

## Mary Rose dela Cruz

Chief Executive Officer

MR’s experience spans 25 years in software development, IT consulting, and global outsourcing. She has served on the boards of the Philippine Software Industry Association, Philippine Science High School System, and Adamson University’s Industry Advisory Board for Computer Science. A techie at heart, MR holds a Bachelor of Computer Science degree from the University of the Philippines and a master’s degree in computing from Macquarie University in Australia.

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Cary.png)

## Cary Santos

Head of Delivery

As head of delivery, Cary brings 20 years of enterprise IT delivery experience to every Stratpoint project. Cary has served in various technological roles in the enterprise IT industry, applying his expertise in process frameworks such as PMBOK 5, CMMi3, and Lean and Green Belt Six Sigma. He currently leads the Agile practice of Stratpoint.

Head of Tech Council

At present, he is also the head of the Tech Council, a group comprised of the company’s leading technical experts, in charting the course for innovation, technical capability building, and tools evaluation and development standards.

## OUR INVESTOR

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Narra.webp "Narra")

Narra Venture Capital  
Investor

Narra Venture Capital is a venture management and advisory group that invests in private high-technology companies with potential for accelerated growth. Established in January 2002, it focuses its investments on companies with defensible barriers to competition — based on cutting-edge technology or their efficient deployment.

In Silicon Valley and similar technology centers, Narra has stakes in companies developing semiconductors, converged communication systems, computing platforms, and software. More recently, it has made smaller investments on promising Internet startups. The firm works with Silicon Valley-based partners like Tallwood Venture Capital.

![](http://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Dado-Banatao.webp "Dado Banatao")

## OUR INSPIRATION

**Diosdado “Dado” Banatao** was a valued investor and adviser at Stratpoint, who passed away in December 2025.

Dado’s life was defined by vision, resilience, and a deep commitment to service. From his humble beginnings in the Cagayan Valley, he became a pioneering force in Silicon Valley—helping shape the foundations of modern computing and inspiring generations of engineers and innovators.

He was equally passionate about giving back, championing education, mentorship, and greater opportunities for Filipino talent through his foundations and initiatives.

We remain deeply grateful for his guidance, wisdom, and belief in our mission. His legacy continues to inspire how we think, build, and serve. We honor the path he paved and the impact he continues to have on our community.

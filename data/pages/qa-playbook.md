# Achieve Software Quality, Today

![](https://stratpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/Stratpoint-QA-Playbook-Laptop.webp "Stratpoint QA Playbook - Laptop")

Turnaround times for apps and software — whether client-facing or internal — have become urgent. You have to develop faster, more amazing apps while maintaining quality and compliance to standards. 

Stratpoint abides by QA policies and standards that we have tested and refined over the years. We have published our strategies in the **Stratpoint QA Playbook**.

In this playbook, you will find:

* The ideal organizational structure for a QA team
* Setting up a QA governance team
* Best practices in testing: from onboarding, test planning, test design, test execution, test reporting, to closure
* Best practices in web, mobile, API, big data testing, and testing in production
* Test Automation, Performance and Volume Testing, and Basic Security Testing processes
* QA working with other teams within the organization

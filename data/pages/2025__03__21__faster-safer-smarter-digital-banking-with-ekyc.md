Faster, Safer, Smarter Digital Banking with eKYC

As digital banking becomes the norm, verifying customer identities securely and efficiently is more crucial than ever. However, traditional Know Your Customer (KYC) processes are slow, costly, and vulnerable to fraud. This leads to regulatory challenges and poor customer experiences, with [48% of banks](https://thefintechtimes.com/kyc-remains-a-largely-manual-process-costing-firms-time-and-money/) globally losing customers due to a slow onboarding process.

According to a [Juniper Research study](https://www.juniperresearch.com/press/regtech-to-account-for-over-50-of-regulatory/), spending on regulatory technology (regtech) solutions, including electronic KYC (eKYC), is expected to reach $204 billion by 2026, signaling the industry’s shift toward digital identity verification.

# **What is eKYC?**

**eKYC** is the digital solution for customer verification, eliminating the need for lengthy paper-based and manual authentication.

* **Biometric authentication:** Fingerprint, facial, or iris recognition ensures a person’s identity is authentic.
* **AI-driven identity verification:** AI analyzes vast data points to detect inconsistencies and prevent fraud.
* **Document verification:** Optical character recognition (OCR) and machine learning extract and validate details from official IDs.
* **Liveness detection:** Prevents spoofing by confirming the presence of a live person.

Unlike traditional KYC, which can take days or weeks, eKYC can verify a customer’s identity in minutes, offering a seamless and secure experience.

# **Benefits of eKYC**

**Faster, frictionless onboarding**

With eKYC, identity verification takes minutes instead of days, reducing drop-offs and enhancing customer experience.

**Robust fraud prevention**

AI-driven anomaly detection helps banks identify fraudulent documents and fake identities before onboarding is complete.

**Seamless regulatory compliance**

Banks can automate compliance with anti-money laundering (AML) and KYC regulations, reducing the risk of hefty fines.

**Cost savings and operational efficiency**

By eliminating manual KYC, banks can cut onboarding costs by [over 70%](https://www.forbes.com/sites/tomgroenfeldt/2018/12/03/reducing-kyc-costs-with-digital-and-artificial-intelligence/) while improving accuracy.

**Higher customer retention**

Faster, seamless digital onboarding leads to higher satisfaction and fewer abandoned applications.

Stratpoint Technologies is Now an AWS MSP Partner

[Stratpoint Technologies](https://stratpoint.com/), a leading digital transformation enabler and IT outsourcing partner in the Philippines and an AWS Advanced Tier Partner, has officially been validated as an [AWS Managed Service Provider (MSP) Partner](https://partners.amazonaws.com/partners/001E000001Alx5gIAB/Stratpoint%20Technologies%20Inc.). This elite designation assures customers of its proven track record and experience in delivering high-quality, end-to-end AWS solutions. The AWS MSP Competency validates Stratpoint’s full-spectrum capabilities, covering every stage of the Cloud journey, including planning, building, operating, and optimization.

The program requires AWS Partners to undergo extensive, rigorous, and multi-year independent audits of their business health and technical processes, proving their ability to manage and optimize mission-critical AWS environments.

For Stratpoint, the achievement is a key milestone that demonstrates its maturity and commitment to delivery excellence. The validation assures clients that they will receive [top-tier managed services](https://stratpoint.com/stratpoint-managed-cloud-aws) for their Cloud infrastructure.

We’re the first homegrown company to achieve this competency, which shows our technical and operational maturity is world-class.

**— MR dela Cruz, CEO of Stratpoint**

The MSP validation confirms that Stratpoint’s team of certified Cloud engineers possesses the highest caliber of AWS technical skills and maintains rigorous Cloud Center of Excellence service operations and business growth.

Achieving this competency status differentiates Stratpoint by proving its ability to implement AWS solutions up to the highest level, bringing deeper business value to the customer. For clients, this means a significantly reduced risk and a smoother journey to the Cloud.

Working with an AWS MSP Partner like Stratpoint gives clients the assurance that their Cloud journey will be smoother, implemented using best practices, and cost optimized.

**— Sonny Carlos, Head of the Cloud and Data Business of Stratpoint**

This proven expertise in optimization, operations, and technical proficiency ensures customers can realize maximum returns on their Cloud investment.

Stratpoint views the AWS MSP Competency as a foundational step in its strategic growth and has no plans to stop or slow down.

The next step for us is to go for AWS Premier Tier Services Partner status to demonstrate our commitment to provide a comprehensive set of Cloud offerings for our clients.

**— MR dela Cruz, CEO of Stratpoint**

## Summary

One of the leading universal banks in the Philippines embarked on a project to revamp its mobile and internet banking solutions to have a unified look, feel, and experience. This aimed to transition its mobile banking services from monolithic architectures to scalable, interoperable microservices by leveraging OutSystems, a low-code platform for rapid development.

The project was successful as it achieved its goal of streamlined business operations, enhanced user experience, and maintained the bank’s leadership in digital innovation.

## Challenges

The bank faced significant hurdles with its monolithic legacy systems, risking prolonged development cycles and missed opportunities for market leadership.

* Existing monolithic architecture hindered seamless integrations.
* Limited in-house expertise hampered the ability to fully leverage OutSystems for its modernization goals.
* There was pressure to roll out new features rapidly to stay competitive and meet evolving customer expectations.

## Solutions

The bank partnered with Stratpoint to execute a tailored modernization roadmap:

* **Microservices transition**
  + Legacy modernization by converting critical backend services from monolithic structures to scalable microservices.
  + Developed an API gateway with OutSystems that optimized request routing to enable seamless communications across multiple digital channels.
* **OutSystems enablement**
  + Utilized low-code capabilities to accelerate mobile app feature development.
  + Delivered innovative functionalities, enhancing the app’s usability and user engagement.
* **Agile development practices**
  + Addressed the bank’s extensive backlog with incremental and value-driven deliveries.
  + Ensured rapid rollouts of essential features to support dynamic market demands.

Stratpoint Becomes Official Katalon Solution Partner to Elevate Test Automation Capabilities in the Philippines

Stratpoint Technologies, a leading digital transformation enabler and IT outsourcing partner in the Philippines, announced its official partnership with global test automation leader [Katalon](https://katalon.com/), becoming a certified [Katalon Solution Partner](https://katalon-inc.my.site.com/partner/s/detail/a1ORA000001dtfC2AQ). This alliance strengthens Stratpoint’s Quality Assurance (QA) capabilities with world-class tools and strategic resources, delivering smarter, faster, and more scalable QA solutions for its clients.

This partnership marks a strategic leap in Stratpoint’s mission to democratize quality engineering by making enterprise-grade automation more accessible across the software development lifecycle (SDLC).

This partnership validates our direction of democratizing quality, making robust test automation tools available to all levels of testers, not just seasoned developers. With Katalon, we can now offer more maintainable, scalable automation frameworks and accelerate digital transformation for our clients.

I’m most excited about low-code test automation, AI-assisted testing, and self-healing scripts, areas where Katalon is already making strides. These trends democratize test automation and allow us to deliver value faster. Katalon also keeps us ahead by actively evolving with market needs, and as partners, we gain early access to features, support, and insights that help us innovate faster for our clients.

**— Lyndon Catalan, Stratpoint’s Head of QA Business**

Katalon, known for its AI-native test automation platform, sees this collaboration as key to expanding its footprint in the Southeast Asian market, particularly in the Philippines.

Stratpoint brings strong technical expertise and a trusted presence in the local technology ecosystem.

As a Solution Partner, they help extend Katalon’s reach by delivering localized support, post-sales services, and scalable automation implementations to our enterprise customers in the region.

**— Shshank Raina, Katalon Regional Alliances Manager, APAC & MEA**

Through this partnership, Stratpoint clients will benefit from tools such as Katalon’s TestOps, TestCloud, and hybrid execution capabilities, empowering QA teams to improve test coverage, speed up go-to-market timelines, and shift-left testing in CI/CD pipelines with ease.

At Stratpoint, we are committed to delivering innovation and performance at scale. Partnering with Katalon gives us a strategic edge in quality engineering that directly benefits the customers and communities we serve.

**— MR dela Cruz, CEO of Stratpoint**

This partnership also paves the way for co-developing test automation accelerators tailored to industries such as banking, fintech, and telco, aligning with Stratpoint’s future roadmap to integrate AI, domain-specific tooling, and intelligent QA solutions.

Stratpoint and Katalon are set to build a stronger, more agile testing ecosystem designed for today’s software demands and tomorrow’s innovations.

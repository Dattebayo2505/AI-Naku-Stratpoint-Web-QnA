Stratpoint Ranks Among the Best in OA500 Top Global Outsourcing Firms

Stratpoint Technologies, a leader in IT outsourcing providing digital transformation and enterprise technology solutions, has been named among the top global outsourcing firms in the [OA500 2025](https://www.outsourceaccelerator.com/oa500/), a definitive ranking of the global business process outsourcing (BPO) industry by [Outsource Accelerator](https://www.outsourceaccelerator.com/), the world’s leading outsourcing marketplace. Stratpoint is included among the [top 500 outsourcing firms](https://www.outsourceaccelerator.com/directory/?p=oa500), selected from over 3,200 firms worldwide.

The OA500 ranking is a result of 12 months of extensive research, analyzing more than 20 objective metadata points to determine the most influential outsourcing providers globally. The [ranking methodology](https://www.outsourceaccelerator.com/oa500/methodology-whitepaper/) considers both a company’s public visibility and quality, evaluating factors such as domain authority, site traffic, employee reviews, LinkedIn engagement, and third-party analysis. This recognition highlights Stratpoint’s excellence in delivering high-quality technology services, including agile software development, quality assurance, Cloud, data, and artificial intelligence.

“We are honored to be recognized in the OA500 2025. This achievement reflects our unwavering commitment to innovation, service excellence, and delivering real business impact to our clients worldwide through globally competitive solutions. This also resonates with the trust our growing list of top local enterprises and global tech companies place in us. We share this recognition with our talented team whose dedication drives our success,” said MR Dela Cruz, CEO of Stratpoint Technologies.

Visit [stratpoint.com](https://stratpoint.com) for more information about Stratpoint Technologies and its services.
